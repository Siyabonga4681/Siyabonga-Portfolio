STAR Reflections (copy these into each artefact card on Wix)

Business Communication — STAR

Situation
I was required to prepare a 10-slide presentation on effective workplace communication as part of the communication module.
Task
My task was to demonstrate both theoretical understanding and practical techniques for verbal and non-verbal communication.
Action
I researched best practices, created slides with clear headings, practiced delivery with peers, and revised based on feedback focusing on tone and body language.
Result
The presentation received positive feedback for clarity and structure. I gained confidence in public speaking and improved how I structure messages for different audiences.

Interview Skills — STAR

Situation
Before the mock interview, I lacked confidence in answering situational and behavioural questions.
Task
I needed to prepare concise, structured answers using the STAR method for common interview scenarios.
Action
I practiced with classmates, drafted sample STAR-format answers for 12 common questions, and recorded myself to evaluate clarity and timing.
Result
I became more confident in structuring answers and reduced filler words. During the mock interview, responses were clearer and better received by the panel.

Mock Interview — STAR

Situation
We conducted a recorded mock interview to simulate a real job interview environment.
Task
My task was to present myself professionally and answer questions convincingly while managing nervousness.
Action
I prepared by researching the role, practiced the STAR answers, rehearsed posture and eye contact, and reviewed peer feedback after the first recording.
Result
The revised recording showed improved tone, better pacing, and clearer examples. Feedback highlighted stronger professionalism and improved confidence.

Professional Networking — STAR

Situation
I had minimal presence on professional networking platforms and wanted to create opportunities to connect with industry professionals.
Task
Create an optimized LinkedIn profile and reach out to at least 5 professionals with a concise networking message.
Action
I updated my headline, summary, uploaded a professional photo, added key skills, and sent personalized connection messages to peers and lecturers.
Result
I gained connections and received responses with advice and resources. My profile views increased and I received an invitation to a career talk.

Workplace Etiquette — STAR

Situation
The course included a workshop on workplace behaviour and appearance expectations.
Task
Collaborate with a group to prepare scenarios demonstrating professional and unprofessional conduct and propose solutions.
Action
We divided scenarios, rehearsed role-plays, created a set of recommended actions, and compiled a short guideline document.
Result
The exercise improved my ability to identify and suggest improvements to workplace behaviours. Group feedback emphasized my contribution to practical solutions.
